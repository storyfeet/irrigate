On Your Turn

  • Explore a Land Tile
  • Take any number of actions
  • Receive Income

    Winning the Game

  • Own and Irrigate 10 Land Tiles
  • Own and Irrigate 1 Goal Tile
  • Earn £50

    Actions

  • Rotate a Land Tile:
    • Yours: Free
    • Unowned: £3
    • Someone Else's:Negotiate
  • Buy a Land Tile:
    • Unowned: £14 - distance x £2
    • Someone Else's:Negotiate

    Multi Action Tax

  • First Action: £0
  • Second Action: + £2
  • Third Action: + £3
  • Fourth Action: + £4
  • ...
